# TechneCS Website

Welcome to the GitHub repository for the official website of TechneCS!

## Mission
Our mission is to provide quality computer science education that is accessible to students from diverse backgrounds. We aim to inspire a love for learning, encourage skill development, and foster an environment where students can have a great time while gaining valuable knowledge.

## Website Features
- **Educational Resources:** Explore a variety of resources and materials to support computer science learning.
- **Community building:** Connect with like-minded individuals, mentors, and fellow learners through our community on discord!
- **Events and Programs:** Stay updated on upcoming events, workshops, and programs designed to enhance computer science education.

## Feedback
We appreciate feedback from the community. If you find issues, have suggestions, or want to get involved, please open an issue or submit a pull request.

Thank you for supporting TechneCS and our mission to empower students through computer science education!
